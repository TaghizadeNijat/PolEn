MacroState Control Room is a real-time macroeconomic policy dashboard that analyzes cross-asset market data to infer hidden economic states such as stress, liquidity, growth, and inflation. Using FRED data, Kalman filtering, regime-switching dynamics, and Monte Carlo simulation, it evaluates possible monetary policy actions like easing, holding, or tightening. The platform presents an interactive view of current macro conditions, projected future scenarios, and risk-adjusted policy recommendations through a modern web dashboard built with FastAPI, Next.js, and TypeScript.
